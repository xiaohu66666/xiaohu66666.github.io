I am currently a graduate student at the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University.

My research interests lie in computer vision and multimodal learning, with a focus on visual grounding, reference expression segmentation, and vision-language models. I am particularly interested in fine-grained visual understanding, cross-modal interaction modeling, and controllable generation under complex scenarios.

During my graduate studies, I have been engaged in research on reference expression segmentation (RES), exploring part-level semantic modeling and multi-granularity reasoning. My work involves integrating large vision-language models with segmentation frameworks to improve robustness and interpretability.
